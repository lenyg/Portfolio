Credit Card Validation

Press the green arrow to get started! This project uses the console to display if the card number is valid. After you enter the card number click the console button!

In this project we created a script that will validate a credit card number. The validator takes the following conditions: Number must be 16 digits, all of them must be numbers
You must have at least two different digits represented (all of the digits cannot be the same)
The final digit must be even
The sum of all the digits must be greater than 16
These card numbers are valid:

9999999988880000
6666666666661666
The following credit card numbers are invalid:

a92332119c011112 invalid characters
4444444444444444 only one type of number
1111111111111110 sum less than 16
6666666666666661 odd final number
